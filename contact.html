Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us directly.

How long does the visa process usually take?

Visa processing times vary significantly depending on the destination country and the type of visa applied for. For example, tourist e-Visas for certain countries can be approved within 48 hours, while US or Schengen visitor visas may take anywhere between 2 weeks to a couple of months. Our consultants will provide an estimated timeline during your initial consultation.

Are flight tickets included in your tour packages?

This depends on the specific package. Our "premium" and "all-inclusive" packages typically include round-trip international flights from major hubs. However, we also offer "land-only" packages where you book your own flights and we handle all accommodations, transport, and tours within your destination. Always check the 'Features' list of the package you are interested in.

What happens if my visa application gets rejected?

While we strictly review all documents to ensure the highest chance of approval, the final decision always rests with the embassy. In the unfortunate event of a rejection, we will thoroughly analyze the refusal letter with you and advise on the next best steps, whether that means reapplying with additional documents or appealing the decision.

Do you offer customized travel packages for families?

Absolutely! While we have popular pre-designed packages, our specialty is creating custom itineraries perfectly tailored to your budget, preferences, and group size. We can easily arrange for kid-friendly resorts, adjoined rooms, and appropriate activities for families of all sizes.
